Honey bees

We currently have two colonies out in our pasture of honey bees. It is critical to understand becoming a bee keeper that there is a lot of up front cost in equipment and will require frequent checkin. They have certainly taught us a lot and are an extremely important part in our gardening and flowers.

Interesting Bee Facts

  • honey bees never sleep
  • polliinate roughly 80% of all fruit, vegetable and seed crops in the U.S.
  • can fly 15 miles per hour
  • cover a 3 mile radius from their hive
  • wings stroke at 11,400 times per minute
  • colonies can have as many as 40,000 to 60,000 bees
  • the colony keeps its queen at 92-93 degrees regardless of outside temp

Benefits of Honey

  • Honey is the only food that includes all substances to sustain life including water
  • never spoils
  • natural sweetner
  • soothes scratchy throats
